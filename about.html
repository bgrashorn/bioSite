About

Lexi was born on September 27, 2001, in Whitefish, Montana. Her father was in the military at the time so her and her family were only there for a few years before moving to Colorado, South Carolina, and then finally settling in Nebraska. Lexi’s family consists of her mom, her dad, and an older brother. Her family has three pets, two dogs and one cat. She went to Kindergarten in South Carolina, otherwise all her schooling has come in Nebraska. She graduated high school from Bellevue West High School in 2019 and currently attends the University of Nebraska-Omaha and is on track to graduated in the spring of 2023 with a degree in Elementary Education. In high school, Lexi was the editor-in-chief of her schools’ yearbook. In this role she oversaw the final product of the yearbook, as well as going out and taking pictures for it. Due to her brother playing baseball all her life, her favorite thing to photograph was sports, particularly baseball. She is a lover of animals. She has had many different pets during her life. Her favorites have been her cats, Gracie, and Bella. She had Gracie while she was growing up in middle school and high school. Lexi got Bella during the middle of the Covid pandemic at Felius Cat Cafe. Lexi always goes out of her way to help people and animals. She plans on getting a job helping others one on one after graduating. She also plans on pursuing a master’s degree in a field that would allow her to do help others.
